About Us

Welcome to PRINT INDIA, where textile artistry and tradition have been our guiding principles for over three decades. With 30 years of experience, we have perfected the craft of bringing fabric to life through vibrant colors and intricate designs. Our journey began with a passion for quality and a commitment to preserving the rich heritage of Indian printing.Founded by our visionary owner, Salam Bhai, PRINT INDIA has grown from a passionate endeavor into a cornerstone of the textile printing industry. His dedication to craftsmanship and innovation continues to inspire our work every day. We pride ourselves on being a trusted partner for businesses that value exceptional quality and meticulous attention to detail.Our expertise covers a range of specialized services designed to meet the creative needs of the modern apparel and accessories market.Saree Printing: We specialize in creating exquisite prints for sarees, transforming them into timeless pieces of wearable art.Custom Sketching: Our talented artists provide bespoke sketching services to develop unique patterns and designs from the ground up.Bag Printing: We extend our printing mastery to accessories like shopper bags and side bags, ensuring your brand's aesthetic is consistent and beautiful.At PRINT INDIA, we don't just print on fabric; we weave stories of artistry and excellence. We are honored to carry our 30-year legacy forward and invite you to collaborate with us to create something truly special.Would you like the tone to be more formal or perhaps focus more on the technical aspects of your printing process?
